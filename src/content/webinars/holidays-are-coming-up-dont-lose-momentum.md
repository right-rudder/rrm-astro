As holidays approach, flight school owners face the risk of losing student enrollment momentum, but this slowdown offers a prime window for strategic digital marketing to outpace stagnant competitors. Right Rudder Marketing’s webinar equips you with proven tactics to maintain lead flow, boost online visibility, and scale enrollments during off-peak periods.
Attendees gain actionable strategies like holiday-themed discovery flight promotions, email nurture sequences for past leads, and SEO optimizations for seasonal searches such as “gift pilot lessons.”
The session emphasizes low-cost, high-ROI moves, including social media content repurposing and PPC for “learn to fly gifts,” helping position your school as the year-round leader.
