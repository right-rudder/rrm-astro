Most flight schools skip marketing fundamentals like UVP, jumping straight to tactics such as PPC or social media, which leads to wasted budgets. This session covers the 3Ms of marketing—Market (who your ideal students are), Message (your UVP that communicates uniqueness), and Medium (channels to reach them)—to attract more enrollments. Attendees gain tools to compete not just with nearby schools but with alternatives like boating or career programs.
• Step-by-step UVP development: Identify what sets your school apart, like modern fleets, experienced instructors, or accelerated programs.
• Real-world exercises: Role-play ideal customer scenarios and distill unique qualities into concise messaging for websites and ads.
• Proven results: Align UVP with sales processes from curiosity to commitment, boosting conversions and student retention
