Are you a flight school owner wondering how much you should actually be investing in marketing? Struggling to decide where to allocate your budget for maximum ROI?
Join Right Rudder Marketing for this essential webinar where we break down exactly how to build a marketing budget that drives real student enrollments and revenue growth in 2027.

What You’ll Learn:
• Budget Fundamentals: Discover the industry-standard formula for setting your marketing budget (5-10% of target revenue) and why investing less than the minimum threshold wastes money
• Channel Prioritization: Learn which marketing channels deliver the highest ROI for flight schools - from SEO and Google Ads to social media and email marketing
• 2027 Marketing Trends: Stay ahead with emerging strategies including AI-powered marketing, video content, and community building that will dominate the aviation industry
• The Flight School Marketing System: Understand how to implement a multi-channel approach that includes website optimization, search engine ranking, PPC advertising, and reputation management
• Cost Per Lead Breakdown: Get realistic expectations for ad spend, cost per click, and conversion rates specific to flight school marketing
• Scaling Strategies: Learn when and how to scale your marketing budget as your school grows from startup to multi-location operation

Whether you’re running a small flight school with 3 aircraft or managing a multi-million dollar operation, this webinar will give you a clear roadmap for allocating your marketing dollars strategically to maximize student enrollments.
