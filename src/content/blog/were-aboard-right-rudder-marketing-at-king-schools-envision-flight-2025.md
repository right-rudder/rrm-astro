At Right Rudder Marketing, we have one core mission: helping flight schools train more pilots. That's it. Everything we do comes back to that purpose.

Sponsoring Envision Flight 2025 is our way of walking the talk. King Schools has been a hall-of-fame institution in aviation education for decades, and when they put together an event focused on advancing flight training, we knew we had to be part of it.

This isn't just about business for us. It's about giving back to the aviation community that's given us so much, strengthening relationships with flight school owners across the country, and learning from each other's challenges and wins.

## Tim Jedrek Takes the Stage

Our founder and CEO, Tim Jedrek, is presenting this week on marketing strategies that actually work for flight schools. Not generic business advice that sounds good but doesn't translate to aviation. Real, proven strategies designed specifically for flight training businesses.

Tim brings a unique perspective to the table. He's both a pilot and a marketing expert with over a decade of business experience. He gets it. He understands the unique challenges flight schools face because he lives and breathes this industry every day.

His presentation covers what we call the **Flight School Marketing System**, the same framework detailed in his book, ["Flight School Handbook of Marketing Knowledge."](/resources/flight-school-handbook) Attendees are getting actionable insights they can implement as soon as they get back to their schools:

- **Building websites that convert.** Your website isn't a brochure. It's your 24/7 salesperson, and it needs to be optimized to turn prospective student pilots into enrolled students.

- **Dominating local search.** When someone in your area searches "flight school near me," you need to show up first. We're talking about SEO strategies that actually move the needle.

- **Social media that builds community.** Aviation is passionate. Your social media should reflect that authenticity and create genuine connections with aspiring pilots.

- **Email marketing that nurtures leads.** Not everyone's ready to book a discovery flight today. But with the right email campaigns, you stay top-of-mind until they are.

- **Data-driven decisions.** We're diving into how to use analytics to measure your marketing ROI and make informed choices about where to invest your marketing budget.

## Why This Matters Now

Let's talk about the elephant in the room: the pilot shortage.

Boeing projects a need for hundreds of thousands of new pilots over the next two decades. That's not a maybe. That's happening. And flight schools are the front line of solving this industry-wide challenge.

But here's the catch: you can't train pilots if you can't reach them. That's where effective marketing comes in.

Every aspiring pilot out there is searching online, checking social media, reading reviews, and comparing flight schools. If your marketing isn't connecting with them, they're choosing someone else. It's that simple.

Right Rudder Marketing exists to make sure that doesn't happen. We help flight schools [fill their training pipelines](/schedule-call) with qualified, motivated student pilots who are ready to pursue their aviation dreams.
